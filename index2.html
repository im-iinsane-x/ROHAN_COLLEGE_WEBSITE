// WHY ROHAN
± LAB ACCESS

Open workshops, not booked slots

CNC bays, fabrication labs, and the electronics workshop stay open till 9pm. Students build outside the syllabus, on their own projects, with raw material budgets from the college.

± FACULTY RATIO

1 mentor per 12 students

Every student is assigned a faculty mentor in year one who tracks their project work, not just attendance, through to final placement.

± INDUSTRY LINK

Live problems, not case studies

Final-year capstones are sourced from manufacturing and infrastructure partners — real constraints, real budgets, real review panels.

Central Fabrication Workshop

CNC machining, welding bays, 3D-print farm, and a materials testing lab shared across all engineering branches.

A2

Structures & Geotech Lab

Full-scale load-testing frame, soil testing bench, and a concrete curing yard for the civil and structural programs.

A3

VLSI & Embedded Design Studio

PCB fabrication, FPGA benches, and a cleanroom-adjacent semiconductor lab for chip-level coursework.

120,000 volumes, IEEE/ASME digital access, and a drafting hall still used for hand-drawn engineering sketches in year one.

Residence Blocks

Six hostel blocks, on-campus medical room, and a 24-hour engineering help desk run by senior students.
